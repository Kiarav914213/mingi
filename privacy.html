Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 16, 2025

At KANTA FOODS INC. we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you interact with us—whether through our website, mobile platforms, physical stores, or at events.

By using our services, shopping with us, or interacting with our website, you agree to the terms outlined below.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

When you purchase from us or sign up for updates, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Billing and shipping address
  • Payment details (handled securely via third-party processors)

b. Non-Personal Information

Collected automatically when you visit our site:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Pages visited and actions taken on our website

c. Offline Information

When you visit our store, attend events, or speak with our team, we may collect:

  • Contact details
  • Purchase history
  • Preferences and feedback

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and deliver orders
  • Provide customer support
  • Send order confirmations, shipping updates, and promotional offers
  • Improve our website, products, and services
  • Personalize your experience both online and offline
  • Detect and prevent fraud or unauthorized activity

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share your information with:

  • Trusted service providers (payment processors, shipping companies, marketing platforms)
  • Law enforcement or government authorities if legally required
  • Business partners for promotional collaborations (only with your consent)

All third parties are required to protect your data and use it only for the purposes outlined in this policy.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies, pixels, and analytics tools (like Google Analytics) to:

  • Understand how visitors use our website
  • Improve user experience
  • Measure the effectiveness of marketing efforts

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Some features may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, including:

  • Legal or regulatory obligations
  • Order and transaction history
  • Customer service or dispute resolution

You may request deletion of your data (see Section 8).

6. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 years of age. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

7. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards (encryption, secure servers, etc.) to protect your data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no system is 100% secure. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login credentials.

8. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct or update your information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of marketing emails (unsubscribe link in emails)
  • Limit data sharing with third parties (as applicable under California law)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@mingicandy.com.

9. California Residents (CCPA Notice)

If you are a California resident, you may request:

  • Disclosure of what categories of personal data we collect and share
  • Deletion of your personal data
  • That we not sell your personal data (we do not sell data)

Please email ccpa@mingicandy.com with “CCPA Request” in the subject line.

10. International Visitors (GDPR Note)

If you’re accessing our website from the EU or UK, you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including:

  • Right to data access
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to data portability

For GDPR-related inquiries, contact gdpr@mingicandy.com.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted here with a new "Effective Date." Your continued use of our services after changes are posted signifies your agreement to the updated policy.
